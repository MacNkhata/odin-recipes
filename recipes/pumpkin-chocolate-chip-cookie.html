Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Cookie

Description

Soft and chewy with a perfect balance of warm pumpkin spices and sweet chocolate, these cookies are a cozy treat for fall. Made with real pumpkin puree, they have a moist texture and rich flavor, complemented by melty chocolate chips in every bite. Perfect for autumn gatherings, holiday baking, or just a sweet indulgence with a cup of coffee or tea.

Ingredients

  • Pumpkin
  • Sugar
  • Oil
  • Egg
  • Leaveners
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Cinnamon
  • Salt
  • Walnuts
  • Chocolate
  • Vanilla

Steps

  1. Combine the wet ingredients. Dissolve the baking soda in milk, then stir it in.
  2. Combine the dry ingredients, then stir the mixture into the wet ingredients.
  3. Stir in the walnuts, chocolate, and vanilla.
  4. Drop the dough onto cookie sheets and bake until the cookies are firm.
